st_nlink¶

Number of hard link

An empty dir is 2:

$ mkdir /tmp/demo
$ ll /tmp
...
drwxr-xr-x  2 iblis   iblis    40 Jul 29 10:32 demo/
...

The 2 located at column 2 is st_nlink.

  • One for the dir itself
  • One for linking to .
