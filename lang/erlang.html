List¶

operators¶

++
right-associative, eval from right to left.
--
right-associative.
> [1,2,3] -- [1,2] -- [3].
[3]

> [1,2,3] -- [1,2] -- [2].
[2,3]

functions¶

hd (head)

> hd([1, 2, 3]).

tl (tail)

pick up [1:]:

> tl([1, 2, 3]).

[2, 3].

> tl([1, 97, 98]). “ab”
